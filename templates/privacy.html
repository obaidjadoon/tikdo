Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TikDo ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our TikTok video downloader service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our service, you provide us with:

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Usage Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Temporary Storage: Downloaded videos are temporarily stored on our servers for up to 1 hour and then automatically deleted

  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • No Personal Data Storage: We do not store personal information beyond what's necessary for service operation
